Read params × next to iso-FLOP ×. The iso-FLOP budget holds compute equal (0.66 EF at 3B tokens) and has a size floor with no ceiling — at equal FLOPs, parameters are the free variable. A per-sequence AdaLN modulation MLP, a top-1 MoE, or a cheaper mixer re-spending its saving all legitimately carry more. A win at 1.00× compute and 5.0× parameters is a consequence of the iso-FLOP budget, not a mechanism result.
